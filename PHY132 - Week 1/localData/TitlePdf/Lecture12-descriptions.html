"Partial Derivatives for Electric Field"
Prof. Hemmick continues the discussion about the relationship between electric potential and electric
eld, and potential energy and force. He introduces partial derivatives in order to solve the relationship
equations.
"Partial Derivatives in Higher Level Coordinate Systems"
Prof. Hemmick introduces taking the partial derivative of the potential with respect to the specic
coordinates of the coordinate system used.
"The Del Operator"
Prof Hemmick generalizes the partial derivative case for each dierent coordinate system, by dening
electric eld as being the negative gradient (del) of Potential. He explains the concept of the del operator
and how it is used with respect to electric eld.
"Neutral Conductor and a Point Charge"
Prof. Hemmick talks about electric eld changing with a point particle and a neutral conductor in empty
space.
"Intro to Capacitors"
Prof. Hemmick introduces the capacitor as two conducting plates of charge Q, describes the electric
eld around such a device using Gauss's Law, and then derives the capacitance formula.
