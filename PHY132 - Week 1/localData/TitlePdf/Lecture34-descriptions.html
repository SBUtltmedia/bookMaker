"Review of Table of Signs"
From last lecture, we review the table of signs for geometrical optics which gives us information about
the signs of the object, the image, focal length, and magnication. Additionally, Prof. Hemmick reviews
the formulas for focal length and magnication.
"Distance - Objects & Images"
Prof. Hemmick presents a few questions to test us on our knowledge of how to calculate the distance of
objects and images for a one and two lens system.
"Moving Our Lenses"
Using the setup from before, Prof. Hemmick moves the second lens and shows what happens to our
object and image distances, as well as the changes in the light's path.
"Outlining a Lens-Mirror Problem"
Prof. Hemmick lays out a problem of light from an object passing through a converging lens, then
reected o a concave mirror. The in and out sides are dened, along with the lens/mirror placement,
radius of mirror curvature, and lens's focal length.
"Solving the Lens-Mirror Problem (part a)"
Using our two formulas (focal length and magnication), along with our table of signs, we go through how
to solve for object and image 1 and 2.
"Solving the Lens-Mirror Problem (part b)"
We continue solving our lens-mirror system, this time for object and image 3.
